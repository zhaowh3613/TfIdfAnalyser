It pays to pay teachers well, Jinhua sets an example
On Teacher's Day, observed on Sept 10, the education department in Jinhua, Zhejiang province, announced that the local government has implemented the regulation that mandates the average salary of teachers cannot be less than that of local civil servants. In fact, media reports say some Jinhua teachers earn more than civil servants; in some areas their year-end bonus is higher than 90,000 yuan ($13,175).
